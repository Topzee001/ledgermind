For this hackathon, the solution we will be building is called LedgerMind — an AI-powered accounting assistant designed to help small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) and freelancers manage their financial records more effectively.

Many small businesses rely on manual bookkeeping methods or spreadsheets, which often lead to inaccurate records, poor expense tracking, and limited visibility into their financial performance. Without clear financial insights, businesses struggle to monitor cashflow trends, prepare for taxes, or demonstrate financial stability when applying for loans or investments.

LedgerMind addresses this challenge by allowing users to upload financial transaction data, automatically categorize expenses using AI or rule-based systems, and visualize their financial performance through dashboards and predictive insights.

The platform will also provide features such as cashflow forecasting for the next three months and a credit readiness score that helps businesses understand their financial health and potential eligibility for loans.

LedgerMind – AI Accounting Assistant for SMEs
Category: AI Accounting SaaS
1. Problem Statement
Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and freelancers often struggle with managing
their financial records effectively. Many businesses rely on manual bookkeeping methods
or spreadsheets, which can lead to inaccurate financial records, poor expense tracking,
and limited visibility into business performance. Without clear financial insights,
businesses struggle to understand cashflow trends, prepare for taxes, or demonstrate
financial stability when applying for loans or investment. LedgerMind solves this problem
by providing an AI-powered accounting assistant that automatically categorizes
transactions and predicts future cashflow.
2. Objectives
• Automate expense categorization for SMEs and freelancers.
• Provide visual dashboards for tracking revenue and expenses.
• Predict short-term cashflow trends.
• Improve financial visibility for small businesses.
• Provide a credit readiness score to help businesses understand loan eligibility.
3. Target Users
• Small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs)
• Freelancers and independent professionals
• Startup founders managing early finances
• Small business accountants
4. Core Features (Hackathon MVP)
• CSV Upload – Upload financial transaction files containing revenue and expenses.
• Auto Expense Categorization – AI or rule-based system categorizes transactions
automatically.
• Financial Dashboard – Visual dashboard showing income vs expenses.
• Cashflow Forecast Chart – Predictive graph estimating the next three months of
cashflow.
• Credit Readiness Score – Score indicating financial health and potential loan
readiness.
5. Optional Enhancements
• Automated tax estimates
• Financial health rating system
• Bank API integrations for automatic data import
• AI financial recommendations
• Invoice tracking and payment reminders
6. Data Inputs
• Revenue transaction entries
• Expense transaction entries
• Transaction dates
• Transaction descriptions
• Uploaded CSV accounting files
7. Expected Output
• Categorized financial transactions
• Monthly income and expense summaries
• Cashflow visualization dashboard
• Three-month financial forecast
• Credit readiness score
8. Demo Requirements (Hackathon)
• User uploads a CSV file with financial transactions.
• System automatically categorizes expenses.
• Dashboard updates instantly with categorized insights.
• Cashflow forecast chart appears.
• Credit readiness score is displayed.
This prototype demonstrates how AI-powered financial tools can simplify bookkeeping and
provide predictive insights for SMEs and freelancers.
